Experience the Legend

The Amazing Thailand Motorcycle Tour offers an exciting and immersive journey through the heart of Thailand, showcasing the stunning landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture of the Land of Smiles. This tour takes you through scenic routes, hidden gems, and iconic landmarks that will leave you with unforgettable memories.

Ride through the famous Mae Hong Son Loop, visit the Golden Triangle where three countries meet, and experience the thrill of the R1148, ranked as one of the top 10 best motorcycle roads in the world.

Tour Highlights

Doi Inthanon: Ride to the highest peak in Thailand.
1,864 Curves: Conquer the legendary road to Mae Hong Son.
Golden Triangle: Boat trip on the Mekong River (Laos border).
White Temple: Visit the stunning Wat Rong Khun.
Bo Kluea Salt Mines: Ancient salt wells in the mountains.
Road R1148: A motorcyclist's dream road.

Daily Itinerary

1

Chiang Mai – Doi Inthanon – Mae Sariang (278 km)

Ride to Thailand's highest peak, 2,565 meters above sea level. Visit waterfalls and Royal Pagodas before descending to Mae Sariang near the Myanmar border.

2

Mae Sariang – Mae Hong Son – Pai (287 km)

Narrow twisty roads to the World War II museum. Visit the longest bamboo bridge in Thailand (Su Tong Pae) and end the day in the hip town of Pai.

3

Pai – Chiang Dao – Fang (270 km)

Visit Pai Memorial Bridge and Chiang Dao Cave. Ride "Little Toscana" mountain roads to Fang with views of Doi Angkhang.

4

Fang – Golden Triangle – Chiang Khong (194 km)

Visit the Long-neck Karen tribe, Doi Mae Salong tea farms, and the Golden Triangle. Take a long-tail boat on the Mekong River.

5

Chiang Khong – Chiang Rai – Nan (328 km)

Visit the famous White Temple (Wat Rong Khun). Ride the exhilarating mountainous highway to Nan.

...

Days 6-10: Nan, Bo Kluea, Phayao, Sukhothai, Lampang & Return

Rest day in Nan, Salt Mines, Magic Route 1148, Sukhothai Historical Park, Elephant Hospital, and return to Chiang Mai.
